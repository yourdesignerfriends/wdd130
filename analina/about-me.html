Hello, I'm Analina (some call me cookie). I like to make connections between people, this is why I decided to study Software Development. In some way I feel that we are all connected and that there is more than one person willing to give you what you need. My job is to help you find those people.

Here's a little about my work experience and skills

When I graduated from high school I was admitted to study medicine at the university. This vocation is strongly linked to the fact that I enjoy helping others.

I realized I know that studying Medicine took up a lot of my time and took me away from my family. Especially my mother, she was a widow and she needed my support to take care of my little brothers. Due to this situation I changed schools and began a career in Public Accounting.

Studying public accounting helped me develop useful skills for my personal and professional life, two of the most important what they are:

1. Working in an orderly and organized manner facilitates transparency.

2. Numbers don't lie.

One of my favorite classes was Economics. My teacher was a little crazy but he was a genius. I learned that an economist is capable of predicting the future and that helped me be more careful and keep in mind that every decision I make will have a consequence.
